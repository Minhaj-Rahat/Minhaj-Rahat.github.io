I am Minhajul Alam Rahat. Currently I am a fourth year PhD student at the *University of Colorado Colorado Springs* Computer Science department. I am working as a research assistant under the supervision of *Drs. Yanyan Zhuang and Gedare Bloom*. My research focus is in *IoT Malware analysis*. My work includes reverse engineering malware binaries, designing automated analysis tools, investigating malware families and investigating machine learning application in malware analysis domain.
